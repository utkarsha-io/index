What is Fake News?

Fake news is false or misleading information presented as real news to confuse or mislead people.

Types of Fake News

  • Clickbait News
  • False Information
  • Manipulated Images or Videos
  • Propaganda
  • Misleading Headlines

How to Identify Fake News

  • Check trusted sources
  • Verify from official websites
  • Read full article
  • Check author and date
  • Do not share without confirmation
